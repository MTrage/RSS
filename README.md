### Quick guide

    RSS [TIME 1] [TIME 2] [TIME 3] [TIME 4] [Mhz Value] [optional with -o]      
                                                                               
### Example 

    RSS 18 15 15 15 3000                                               

#### If you enter -o at the end of the values, you will only get the RSS value!
